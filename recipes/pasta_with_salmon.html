Pasta with Salmon Sauce

Description

Here we have a very simple yet delicious recipe for pasta - normally spaghetti - with a perfect salmon sauce that will blow your mind.

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice
  • Whipped cream (or a vegan alternative)
  • Salmon (deepfried or fresh)
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Soup Powder
  • Lemon Juice
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Put the salmon in the oven until it's cooked tender, and cook the pasta at the same time
  2. While the salmon is in the oven, cut the onions in small stripes and roast them
  3. When the salmon is ready, break it up into smaller pieces and add it to the onions
  4. Season it with salt and pepper
  5. Add some soup powder
  6. Add your whipped cream
  7. Let it cook for a few minutes
  8. Add some lemon juice to finish it off
  9. Finally, mix the pasta with your sauce
  10. Enjoy!
